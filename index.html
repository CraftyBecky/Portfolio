About Me

I am a developer working out of the Denver Metro area. I started my career by serving in the United States Air Force Reserves, while at the same time learning and working in more creative and technical fields such as custom picture framing and medical billing. I am now combining all of my collective experience with my coding skills to deliver problem-solving solutions that are creative, well thought out, and scalable.

Being able to create things with code is both challenging and enjoyable. I love to take a design thought and turn that thought out with code. Solving those problems and bugs that come up is extremely satisfying. I also find myself interested in learning UI/UX to further improve upon my ability to produce the best user experience.

In my free time I enjoy games of all sorts (board, video and roleplaying), serving the Denver Metro area through volunteer work within the local community, working conventions with my Star Trek fan organization, and finding time for outdoor activities. I have a wide range of interests from fountain pens and journaling, to Star Trek and Stranger Things.
